Our Causes: Entrepreneurship

Driving Economic Growth and Job Creation on Mfangano Island

Revenue-Generating Opportunities

Objective: Evaluate and implement sustainable business opportunities to drive economic growth and job creation.

  • Fee-Based Public Toilets: Establish clean and accessible public toilets at beach landing sites.
  • Cold Storage Facilities: Build facilities to support fishermen and local food vendors.
  • General Contractor Services: Offer electrician, maintenance, and repair services.
  • Proper Piers: Develop piers for waterbus and private boat docking.
  • Business Center Complex: Create a hub for small businesses and entrepreneurs.
  • Real Estate Rentals: Provide housing for Sub-County employees and teachers.

Fee-Based Public Toilets

Objective: Improve sanitation and generate revenue at beach landing sites.

  • Construct and maintain clean, fee-based public toilets.
  • Ensure accessibility for tourists and locals.
  • Generate income to fund community projects.

Cold Storage Facilities

Objective: Support fishermen and food vendors with reliable cold storage solutions.

  • Build facilities to preserve fish and perishable goods.
  • Reduce post-harvest losses and increase income for local businesses.
  • Create jobs in facility management and maintenance.

General Contractor Services

Objective: Provide essential maintenance and repair services for the community.

  • Offer electrician, plumbing, and general repair services.
  • Train and employ local youth in skilled trades.
  • Ensure affordable and reliable services for residents.

Proper Piers for Waterbus and Private Boats

Objective: Improve docking facilities for waterbuses and private boats.

  • Construct safe and durable piers at key locations.
  • Enhance transportation and tourism opportunities.
  • Generate revenue through docking fees.

Business Center Complex

Objective: Create a hub for small businesses and entrepreneurs.

  • Provide office spaces, meeting rooms, and shared facilities.
  • Offer training and mentorship programs for startups.
  • Foster innovation and collaboration among local businesses.

Real Estate Rentals

Objective: Provide affordable housing for Sub-County employees and teachers.

  • Develop rental properties in strategic locations.
  • Ensure quality and affordability for tenants.
  • Generate steady income for community development.

Future Plans

Vision: A thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem on Mfangano Island.

  • Expand revenue-generating projects to cover more sectors.
  • Attract investors and partners for sustainable development.
  • Create more jobs and improve the quality of life for residents.
