MDF PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION, LEGAL AFFAIRS AND SECURITY

Public, Administration Legal Affairs & Security (Convernor: Michael Anganyo)

To handle all Public Administration, Legal, and Security matters affecting the greater Mfangano Islands, to include the security of the land and lake from within and external players. The committee will spearhead activities with the NG SubCounty official, the County representative, and the other apparatus of the State.
