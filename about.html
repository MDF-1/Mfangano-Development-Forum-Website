ABOUT MDF

Overview

The Mfangano Development Forum (MDF) is a community-based organization dedicated to fostering inclusive prosperity, cohesion, and peace on Mfangano Island. Established in 2021, MDF works across various sectors to improve the lives of the island's residents.

History

MDF was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate individuals committed to addressing the challenges faced by the Mfangano community. Over the years, we have grown into a leading force for development on the island.

Our Structure

MDF operates through a well-defined structure that includes a board of directors, various committees, and a dedicated team of staff and volunteers. This structure ensures efficient implementation of our programs.

Our Foundation

Our foundation is built on the principles of community empowerment, transparency, and sustainability. We believe in working hand-in-hand with the community to achieve lasting change.
