Our Causes: Power

Empowering Mfangano Island with Reliable and Sustainable Energy

KPLC Generator

Objective: Ensure reliable power supply for Mfangano Island.

  • New Generator: A brand-new MPMC 500KVA generator was installed on January 29th, 2025.
  • Additional Generators: 2x500KVA enclosed diesel generators were installed at the Mfangano Power Station.
  • Acknowledgements: Special thanks to the late Convenor Caleb Ginarah, Convenor Walter Mtindi, and KPLC General Manager Eng. Geofrey Muli for making this a reality.
  • Community Gratitude: The arrival of the generator marks the end of a long-awaited journey, "Olugendo Lwifu".

Solar Power for Educational Institutions

Objective: Provide sustainable solar power to all educational institutions on Mfangano Island.

  • Project Scope: Solar power installations for all public primary schools, secondary schools, and adult education centers.
  • Cost: Ksh 120 million plus VAT, to be deployed in phases over 5 years.
  • Funding Request: Annual allocation of Ksh 26.4 million (including 10% annual cost adjustments) plus VAT.
  • Private Schools: Discounted group quotes provided by the installer.
  • Status: Proposal submitted to NG-CDF office and delivered to Hon. MP Millie in June 2024. Follow-up ongoing.

Community Impact

Objective: Improve the quality of life through reliable and sustainable energy solutions.

  • Ensure uninterrupted power supply for homes, businesses, and institutions.
  • Reduce reliance on fossil fuels and promote clean energy.
  • Support education by providing reliable power to schools.
  • Create opportunities for economic growth and development.

Future Plans

Vision: A fully powered and sustainable Mfangano Island.

  • Expand solar power projects to cover more institutions and households.
  • Advocate for increased funding and support from government and private partners.
  • Promote awareness and education on the benefits of clean energy.
  • Develop partnerships with renewable energy organizations for technical and financial support.

Testimonials

Objective: Share the impact of reliable power on the community.

  • School Principal: "The solar power installation has transformed our school. We now have reliable electricity for our classrooms and computer lab."
  • Local Business Owner: "With the new generator, my business no longer suffers from power outages. I can serve my customers better."
  • Community Leader: "The arrival of the KPLC generator is a dream come true for our island. We are grateful to everyone who made this possible."
