MDF HEALTH AND SANITATION

Health and Sanitation (Convenor: Dennis Otieno)

To take the lead on issues pertaining to Health and Sanitation in Mfangano Island and achieve the goals of increased access to diagnostics and curative services; reduce the impact of communicable diseases to include improve awareness non communicable diseases; and support sustainable infrastructure development.
