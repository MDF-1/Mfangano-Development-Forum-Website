Our Causes: Environment

Protecting and Enhancing Mfangano Island's Natural Resources

Water Treatment & Storage

Objective: Establish a major water treatment and storage plant up the hills within the island.

  • Provide clean, safe water for homes, irrigation, and institutions (educational and health).
  • MDF to collaborate with county officials for proper planning and execution in FY 2025/2026.
  • Ensure sustainable water management for future generations.

Forestry & Fire Prevention

Objective: Protect and restore Mfangano Island's forests through community-led initiatives.

  • Establish Community Forest Associations (CFAs) in 5 regions: Wakinga, Soklo, Wakula, Wasamo, and Kakimba.
  • Promote tree planting and reforestation efforts.
  • Mitigate against accidental forest fires through awareness and prevention programs.
  • Collaborate with Kenya Forest Service (KFS) and the Ministry of Lands to mark and protect gazetted forest areas.

Environmental Awareness

Objective: Educate and empower the community to protect the environment.

  • Organize workshops and training sessions on sustainable practices.
  • Promote waste management and recycling programs.
  • Encourage community participation in environmental conservation efforts.

Future Plans

Vision: A sustainable and thriving environment for Mfangano Island.

  • Expand water treatment facilities to cover the entire island.
  • Increase forest cover by 20% over the next 5 years.
  • Develop eco-tourism initiatives to promote environmental conservation.
  • Partner with national and international organizations for funding and expertise.
