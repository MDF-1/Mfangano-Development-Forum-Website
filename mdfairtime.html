MDF Airtime Cashback Program

Earn Cashback for MDF Every Time You Buy Airtime!

Program Overview

MDF has partnered with Tinka to offer a Cashback benefit for all pre-paid airtime purchases made via Mpesa Paybill 151515. Every time you buy airtime, MDF earns a 4% commission on your transaction value.

Example: If the average spend on airtime per user is Ksh 400 per month, MDF earns Ksh 16 per user. With 329 members, this could generate Ksh 5,264 monthly for MDF-CBO.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Use Mpesa Paybill 151515 to purchase pre-paid airtime for Safaricom, Airtel, or Telkom. (Post-paid plans are not eligible.)
  • Step 2: Enter your registered mobile number as the account number.
  • Step 3: MDF earns a 4% Cashback on every transaction, which is instantly processed and transferred to the MDF Cashback Account.
  • Step 4: New members can join by texting "Join-MDF" to Tinka at 0721747747.

Program Benefits

  • Instant Cashback: Earn Cashback on every airtime purchase.
  • Transparent Reporting: Receive an SMS confirmation showing your contribution and the total Cashback amount.
  • Monthly Reports: The MDF Treasurer can generate detailed reports, including participating and non-participating members.
  • Easy to Join: Simply text "Join-MDF" to Tinka to be added to the program.

Program Management

  • Manager: The MDF Treasurer is the board-appointed manager of the Cashback Account.
  • Privacy: Only mobile numbers are shared with Tinka. No names or additional information is provided.
  • Monitoring: All transactions are logged for transparency and accountability.
