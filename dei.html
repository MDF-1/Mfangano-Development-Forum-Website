We Are One

The Absasuba we are dispersed across countries, in Kenya alone we tag five (5) lands. We have sub clans, then clans yet we are ONE. There is only One Mfangano Island. As such, we embrace the diversity of clans, the equity in educating both genders, and inclusion that anyone, from any of the lands, should be a part of everything we do at MDF.

There are different paths for anyone to be involved: Be it through the core MDF - Community Based Organization; be it through the MDF SACCO; or be it through the Investment Vehicles we will be creating. We are committed to having you participate one way or another.

“We are clan- and tribal-minded in that we are communal in our concept of life. A person is not only an individual, he is part of a system, part of a community. The person has a responsibility to the community, and in turn, the community has responsibilities to him and his family. It is what one can call a Mutual Social Responsibility between the person and his community.” Tom Joseph Mboya - a son of Suba

Our Values

At MDF, we celebrate that everyone has God-given talent, and these can be channeled towards a common Good in the true meaning of the African Traditional Concept. We value diversity and celebrate the contributions of all, as well as differences in ideas, thoughts, values, and beliefs.

Diversity

We embrace the uniqueness of every individual and clan.

Equity

We ensure equal opportunities for all, regardless of gender or background.

Inclusion

We create spaces where everyone feels valued and heard.
