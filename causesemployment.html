Our Causes: Employment

Creating Opportunities for Mfangano Island's Workforce

Employment Achievements

Objective: Secure employment opportunities for Mfangano Island residents.

  • Successfully appointed and employed members of MDF and Awivuangano in key positions:
    • 1 Chief Officer
    • 1 Director
    • 8 Clerks
  • Advocated for fair representation in county staffing plans.
  • Ensured opportunities align with the 30% quota reserved for Women, Youth, and Persons with Disabilities.

Future Plans

Vision: Expand employment opportunities for Mfangano Island residents.

  • Engage the Governor to ensure Mfangano receives a fair share of staffing opportunities within the Sub County.
  • Advocate for the 30% quota for Women, Youth, and Persons with Disabilities in all county employment plans.
  • Collaborate with county officials to identify and create new job opportunities.
  • Provide training and skill development programs to prepare residents for employment.

Employment Advocacy

Objective: Advocate for equitable employment opportunities for all residents.

  • Work with county leadership to ensure Mfangano Island is prioritized in staffing plans.
  • Promote inclusivity by ensuring opportunities for Women, Youth, and Persons with Disabilities.
  • Organize community forums to discuss employment challenges and solutions.

Training and Skill Development

Objective: Equip residents with the skills needed for employment.

  • Offer vocational training programs in high-demand fields.
  • Provide mentorship and career guidance to job seekers.
  • Partner with local businesses and organizations for internships and job placements.

Success Stories

Objective: Celebrate the achievements of Mfangano Island residents.

  • Highlight stories of individuals who have secured employment through MDF initiatives.
  • Showcase the impact of employment on families and the community.
  • Inspire others to pursue opportunities and contribute to the island's development.
