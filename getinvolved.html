How You Can Get Involved

We seek and celebrate contribution of skills, talents, and expertise from all. This is essential as we are a volunteer organization, harnessing these gifts of time and ideas towards realizing our shared vision of what is to become Mfangano Island. We now humbly request for you to join, any one of the below listed subcommittee that matches your interest area, and contribute you skills, your talents, towards ONE MFANGANO

Educational and Vocational Training (Convenor: Peter Agwaro)

To improve the educational standards of Mfangano Island by working closely with the relevant public and private sector players in monitoring and reviewing educational and vocational training matters related to Mfangano Island. This is a data driven activity that provides reports, recommendations, and advise for the good of key players and importantly the Mfangano Island fraternity.
