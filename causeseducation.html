Our Causes: Education

Empowering the Future of Mfangano Island Through Education

ECD Classrooms

Objective: Expand Early Childhood Development (ECD) facilities across Mfangano Island.

  • FY 2024/2025: Construct 4 ECD classrooms.
  • FY 2025/2026: Construct 6 additional ECD classrooms.
  • FY 2026/2027: Construct 4 more ECD classrooms.
  • Post a second ECD supervisor on the island within the week.
  • Transfer the existing ECD Director to improve oversight and support.

Improvement in Learning Materials

Objective: Ensure students have access to quality learning materials.

  • Increase the supply of textbooks, stationery, and other learning resources.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to provide modern teaching aids.
  • Regularly assess and address gaps in material availability.

County Government Scholarships

Objective: Support students in achieving their academic and vocational goals.

  • Fins to Swim Program: Fully sponsors 6 students per ward up to university level.
  • Genowa Fundi Mangula Program: Sponsors 15 students per year in vocational training.
  • Provide mentorship and career guidance to scholarship recipients.

Vocational Training

Objective: Equip youth with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

  • Expand vocational training centers on the island.
  • Offer courses in trades such as carpentry, plumbing, tailoring, and IT.
  • Partner with local businesses for internships and job placements.

Future Plans

Vision: A well-educated and skilled community driving Mfangano Island's development.

  • Increase the number of ECD classrooms to cover all regions of the island.
  • Introduce digital learning tools and internet connectivity in schools.
  • Expand scholarship programs to support more students.
  • Develop partnerships with national and international organizations for funding and expertise.
